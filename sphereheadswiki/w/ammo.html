Ammunition

Overview

There are four common types of ammunition, all of which most guns use. However, there are guns that do not use foreign ammo (uses special ammo) and instead only uses reserved ammo integrated within themselves.

Common Ammunition Types

Light Ammunition

Light ammunition stacks up to 90 per slot.

It is typically used for weapons with higher fire rates.

Medium Ammunition

Medium ammunition stacks up to 60 per slot.

It is typically used for weapons with higher damage output.

Shell Ammunition

Shell ammunition stacks up to 18 per slot.

It is strictly for shotguns.

Heavy Ammunition

Heavy ammunition stacks up to 12 per slot.

It is strictly for sniper rifles.

Special Ammunition Types

Nova 50 Rocket Ammunition

This is a special ammunition and only exists as integrated reserve ammunition for Nova 50.

It is explosive.

Archmortis .50 Cal Heavy Ammunition

This is a special ammunition and only exists as integrated reserve ammunition for Archmortis .50 Cal.

It has an ability to instantly kill entities on headshot (375 headshot)
