Guns

Overview

Guns are one of the main ways of dealing damage to other entities.

Below is the list of gun types and their typical descriptions:

  • Pistols (HG) provide the highest mobility and allows to be switched into quickly.
  • Assault Rifles (AR) fire high-damage projectiles and excel in longer ranges.
  • Submachine Guns (SMG) fire projectiles in a rapid succession and are for quick and close-range combat.
  • Shotguns (SH) fire multiple shots at a time and are usually devastating at close range.
  • Sniper Rifles (SR) fire high-damage and sometimes devastating projectiles at long-distance targets.
  • Marksman Rifles (MR) are sufficient for ammo conservation.
  • Heavy Weapons (HV) deal massive damage at the cost of mobility.
