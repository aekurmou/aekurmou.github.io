Circumstances

I am neurodivergent and may forget about commissions, so don't hesitate to remind me about it. Turnaround time may vary depending on my situation.

Midnight (CT) is my most active time of a day.

Due to my drawing experience having most of its focus on bugs, I cannot guarantee any accuracy when it comes to drawing anything outside my preferred character species.



For claiming a slot, the best way to reach out to me is through Discord DM's or by momentarily joining my workaround Discord server.

Do not send a simple "Hi" or "Hello," as I assume these do not have anything to discuss and then ignore. Payment is not upfront; The client must wait until told to initiate payment. Once you and I have completely described the work, I send a very rough sketch, and you agree, pay via Ko-fi before I continue. You can withdraw your slot at any time unless you have paid.
